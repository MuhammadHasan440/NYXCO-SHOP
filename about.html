Our Story

Built on Trust

NYXCO Exchange started with a simple mission: provide a trusted marketplace for luxury streetwear enthusiasts to buy, sell, and trade pieces.

From Passion to Business

What began as a personal collection quickly evolved into something bigger. Based in Hammond, Louisiana, we recognized the need for a reliable source of luxury streetwear in the community.

Every piece in our collection is hand-selected, thoroughly inspected. We do not just sell products—we curate experiences for collectors who understand the value of genuine craftsmanship.

With over 800 successful transactions, we have built our reputation on transparency, fair pricing, and exceptional customer service. Whether you are looking for that grail piece or trying to trade up, we are here to make it happen.
