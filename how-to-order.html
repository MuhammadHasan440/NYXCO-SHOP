Simple Process

How To Order

No complicated checkout. Just screenshot, DM, and deal. We make buying luxury streetwear simple and secure.

1

Screenshot Item

Browse our collection and screenshot the item(s) you are interested in purchasing or trading.

2

Send DM

Message us on Instagram or WhatsApp with the screenshot. We typically respond within 10 minutes.

3

Get Price

We will send you the price, condition details, and additional photos. Open to reasonable offers.

4

Make Payment

Pay via Bank Transfer, CashApp, or Zelle. Once confirmed, your item is reserved and packaged.

5

Receive Item

We ship nationwide with tracking within 48 hours. Local delivery available in Hammond, LA.
